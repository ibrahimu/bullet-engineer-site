Common questions

  • I lost my progress. Progress is stored on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled and restore a new device from that backup, your progress returns. A clean reinstall may reset progress.
  • How do I earn CHIPS? Clear contracts, survive Vault waves, complete daily missions and the Weekly Gauntlet, and claim your daily login reward.
  • The game is one shot — is that a bug? No! Bullet Engineer is a deterministic ricochet puzzle. Every room is solvable with skill — no luck, only skill.
  • Accessibility. Settings include Reduced FX, Large HUD, Type Labels, and toggles for sound, music, haptics, and screen shake.
